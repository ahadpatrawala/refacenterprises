Some of our products

Air-cooled Condenser Units


We can provide customised as well as OEM replacement condenser in V shaped, U type coils even Full O coils for Air Conditioning and Refrigeration units. We manufacture coils that ensure excellent heat transfer capabilities and satisfy limited machine height. Available in both aluminium and copper fin configurations, we have manufactured condenser coils in a wide variety of sizes. Our manufacturing capabilities include tube wall thickness from .030 to .049 inches, tube diameters of 5/16, 3/8, and 1/2, or 5/8 inches, and fin thickness from .013 to .025 inches. With customised design of new and replacement condenser coils, we produce quality coil as per your specific needs. By reproducing existing OEM design condenser coils or creating a new design that can maintain an exact fitment and manufacture a coil based directly on your specifications. We provide a guaranteed quality service to extend the longevity of existing coils and provide the best solutions for chilled water evaporator and DX coil.

Air Handling Units for General and Marine Industry

As our air handling units are used in the marine industry, it is essential that they are made of high-quality materials to withstand the extreme sea climate conditions. The casing is made of galvanized steel or stainless steel and has an aluminium frame with double-walled panels made of galvanized steel (stainless steel as option). The panels are internally insulated with thermal insulation of 25/45/65 mm thickness. There is a direct-driven or belt-driven centrifugal fan, depending on the required air quantity and air pressure. It can also be provided with a plug fan to save space.

Rooftop Self Contained Units

Our roof units are specially designed to provide a compact but efficient cooling solution. Autonomous and easy to maintain, our units are available in a wide range of capacities to meet the individual needs of your installation. Rooftop units are the ideal choice for commercial or industrial installations where interior space may be limited. These units are lightwight and quiet; perfect for use on today's building structures with minimal noise. With nominal cooling capacities ranging from 5 TR to 25 TR. We have supplied these to Pioneer Refrigeration and Air Conditioning work to be installed on marine platforms.

Air washers

Available in both Single and Double Skin Air washer Units comprising inlet louvers, Filter section humidification media with option of cellulose pad or PVC pads, water distribution system, Water eliminators of 4,6 or 8 Passes, pump, Centrifugal Fan, Motor, SS Water Tank. The humidification media consist of corrugated sheets of wettable, strong Cellulose material or PVC Pads with decay resisting chemical and Built in eliminators. These units are available from 5000CMH to 250,000CMH And temperature drop with respective atmosphere is 8 to 10 deg centigrade. Specially Designed Air washers Installed at Parle Products Pvt. Ltd in BHUJ (Gujarat) and Khopoli ( Maharashtra) having air flow capacity of 40000 CFM and 30000 CFM in 2015 reduced the internal air temperature by 12-13 degrees Celsius from ambient temperature.
